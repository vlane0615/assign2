Get to know Vanessa

Long story short

Vanessa Zellmer Lane was born and raised in the metro Milwaukee area in southeastern Wisconsin. Growing up, Vanessa was always equal parts dreamer, problem-solver, and protestor. Heavily shaped by both her religious faith and family, Vanessa decided to pursue a career as a middle school teacher.

Family

While in college in Wisconsin, Vanessa started dating her friend, Marcus. It quickly became serious, and they married in June of 2012. Vanessa and Marcus have been blessed with two children: Della (age 6) and Jude (age 3).
